When no initial appointment is needed
Select no appointment needed if the community probation team have confirmed that this person is being released in the final third of their sentence and is not:
  • MAPPA eligible
  • named on a child protection plan
  • sentenced for a state threat or a terrorist risk offence
  • tier A, B or C
