Prison will create this licence

Pentonville (HMP) will create a licence for this person as none was submitted in time for their final release checks.

This licence must be reviewed after this person is released.

Initial appointment

If you need Pentonville (HMP) to set the initial appointment for a specific date and time or if no appointment is required, you must contact them to request this. You can email test@test.gov.uk

Otherwise, the prison can schedule the appointment for immediately after release or by 2pm the next working day.

When no initial appointment is needed
An initial appointment is not needed if this person is being released in the final third of a standard determinate sentence and is not:
  • MAPPA eligible
  • named on a child protection plan
  • sentenced for a state threat or a terrorist risk offence
  • tier A, B or C
  • serving a standard determinate sentence plus

Licence conditions

The licence will contain standard licence conditions. By default, it will also automatically contain an additional condition.

Making or maintaining contact with a person
Not to approach or communicate with any victims of your offences without the prior approval of your supervising officer.

The prison cannot add any other additional or bespoke conditions.
If you need to add any additional or bespoke licence conditions, you can do so after release.
