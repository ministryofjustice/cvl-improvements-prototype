CRD licence

There are two versions of the prototype flow you can use to show the creation of a CRD licence, one with example additional and bespoke conditions and one without.

Links to start flow (starting from PP in CVL create journey):

  1. Start - flow with example additional conditions (Electronic monitoring conditions 2 and 3) and a bespoke condition

    To use this version, choose 'Yes' on additional conditions and bespoke conditions.

  2. Start - flow with no additional or bespoke conditions

    To use this version, choose 'No' on additional conditions and bespoke conditions.
